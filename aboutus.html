Who We Are
"Team Trees" is an initiative started by YouTubers MrBeast (Jimmy Donaldson) and Mark Rober in October 2019. The initiative gained immense popularity and support across various social media platforms. The primary goal of Team Trees is to combat climate change by planting trees around the world.The main mission of Team Trees is to raise funds to plant trees. The initial goal was to plant 20 million trees, and the campaign aimed to achieve this by encouraging people to donate $1 for every tree planted.Team Trees collaborated with various organizations, environmental groups, and influencers to maximize its impact. The campaign became a global movement with support from people around the world.

Vision
Team Trees envisions a global community united in the pursuit of a sustainable and thriving planet. We see a world where individuals, organizations, and governments work together to combat climate change, promote environmental stewardship, and create a future where nature and humanity coexist harmoniously.
Mission
Team Trees is on a mission to make a positive and lasting impact on the environment. Through the power of collective action, we aim to plant trees on a massive scale, contributing to global reforestation efforts. Our mission is to raise awareness about the critical role of trees in maintaining ecological balance, combating deforestation, and mitigating the impacts of climate change. By fostering a sense of environmental responsibility and leveraging innovative solutions, we strive to create a greener and healthier world for present and future generations.
